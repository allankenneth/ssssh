About

This site is not affiliated with the City of Victoria, British Columbia in any way and is not guaranteed to be accurate. The logic is based on a reading of the bylaws and a customized JSON representation derived from them. You can view the code on GitHub .

Ssssh! was created in the spirit that more people would follow the bylaws and be respectful of their neighbors if they had an easy way to check the bylaws that didn't involve reading a 20 page PDF and attempting to parse out the bits relevant to them in the context of the current date and time that they are checking.
